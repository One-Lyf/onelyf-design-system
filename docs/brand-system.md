# OneLyf + Liv — brand system & taglines

Reference guideline for the OneLyf design system. The authoritative spec is the
v3 "Root Glyph" brand brief; the visual boards ship as the `RootGlyphBoard` and
`BrandIdentityBoard` reference cards.

## The load-bearing rule

**One glyph, split by STATE, not symbol.**

- **Crest = OneLyf** — static, engraved. The home / ownership / lineage mark.
  Present and complete *with the AI off* (OneLyf must work with Liv dormant).
- **Live = Liv** — the same mark, junctions lit warm amber. The intelligence
  woven through the network. *"OneLyf is the home; Liv makes it glow."*

## Taglines (from the brand boards)

**Lead lines**
- The Home. The Network. The Legacy.
- We don't build apps. We cultivate a home for your life.
- A family ecosystem that grows with you.

**OneLyf ↔ Liv duality**
- One glyph. Two states. One meaning.
- One crest. Two states. One home.
- The intelligence that lives within.

**Value props (micro-taglines)**
- Rooted Ownership · Connected Intelligence · Private By Design · Grows With Your Family · Built To Last

## Style directions explored

- **A — Modern Homestead:** warm, family-focused, softer.
- **B — Field Journal:** field journal, paper, ink, handcrafted.
- **C — Living Atlas:** editorial, sophisticated, system-level.

## Aesthetic guardrails

- **Keep:** Field Notes / botanical-journal / atlas / heirloom-paper / museum-catalog warmth.
- **Avoid:** SaaS aesthetics, AI gradients, glowing neon, futuristic robot-assistant UI, crypto visuals, generic startup branding.
- **Device-agnostic / post-mobile** — works for voice, ambient displays, wearables, large screens.
- **AI-optional, family-safe, privacy-visible** — consent shown as connections; no dark patterns.
